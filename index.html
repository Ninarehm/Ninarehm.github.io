Ninareh Mehrabi

Hi! This is Ninareh Mehrabi. I am an applied scientist at Amazon AGI working on developing responsible AI systems. I was previously a postdoctoral researcher at Amazon Alexa AI's Responsible AI team. Prior to that I was a Ph.D. candidate at University of Southern California's Information Sciences Institute where I worked with Prof. Aram Galstyan and Fred Morstatter as part of the MINDS group.
